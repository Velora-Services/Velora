Legal

Booking & Cancellation Policy

Platform Role

VELORA is a marketplace intermediary. We connect clients with independent professionals. All bookings are sent directly to the provider — no VELORA approval step is required. Final terms are agreed between client and professional.

VELORA facilitates discovery and connection. We are not party to the contract between client and provider and are not responsible for the quality, delivery or outcome of any service.

Booking Process

  • Clients submit a booking request selecting a specific professional.
  • The professional is notified immediately via email and dashboard.
  • The booking is confirmed once the professional accepts and contacts the client.
  • All payments are handled directly between client and professional — no transactions occur through VELORA.
  • VELORA is always free for customers. No hidden fees. No subscriptions. Ever.

Cancellation — Clients

  • More than 48 hours before event: No penalty. Notify the professional as early as possible.
  • Less than 48 hours before event: A cancellation fee may apply at the professional's discretion.
  • No-show or same-day cancellation: The professional reserves the right to charge a full or partial fee.

Cancellation — Service Providers

  • Providers must notify the client immediately if they become unavailable.
  • Providers are expected to offer a suitable replacement or reasonable compensation.
  • Repeated cancellations without adequate notice may result in suspension or removal from VELORA.
  • Cancellation within 24 hours requires best efforts to source a replacement professional.

Provider Plans & Fees

VELORA offers a free Basic listing for all providers. Optional Featured ($29/mo) and Premium ($59/mo) plans provide enhanced visibility. No commission is ever charged on your earnings. Clients never pay a platform fee.

Privacy

Information submitted through VELORA booking and join forms is used solely to facilitate the connection between clients and professionals. We do not sell personal data to third parties. By submitting a form you consent to your details being shared with the relevant professional.
